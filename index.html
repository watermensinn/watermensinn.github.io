About Us

The Watermen's Inn is a casual gourmet dining experience. We use fresh, local and seasonal seafood, fruits and vegetables. Our meat is hand-cut and grilled, broiled or sautéed to perfection. Our nightly dinner specials amplify the regular menu and are prepared with a combinations of sauces, herbs and salsas to complement each unique entrée. A display just inside the door reveals an array of mouthwatering homemade desserts. For a quick bite & cocktail, step into our adjacent tap room and sample our fine selection of wine, cold draft beers or classic martinis.

Know our History

Brian Julian and Kathy Berezoski have owned & operated The Watermen's Inn in Crisfield, Maryland since the summer of 1988. Both have Associate Degree's in Culinary Arts from Johnson & Wales University in Providence, Rhode Island. Kathy has an additional Associate Degree in Pastry Arts and a Bachelor's Degree in Food Service Management. After graduating in the early 1980's, Brian and Kathy have each had several culinary experiences along the eastern seaboard before returning to Kathy's home on the Eastern Shore.
