About Us

From corporate breakfast to sit down dinners, from bridal showers to "cutting the cake", the Watermen's Inn professional and courteous staff will attend to your every need. We'll work with you to customize your menu and will provide you with the best fresh ingredients possible to make your meal a memorable one. The gourmet tastes of the Watermen's Inn can literally be brought to your door with our catering services.

Know our History

Over the years we have worked in many private homes and outdoor venues in the area. We are on the preferred catering list at the Pocomoke Elks, and have prepared countless meals at the Ocean City Golf & Yacht Club, the Merry Sherwood Plantation, Dove Pointe in Salisbury, the Ward Museum and the Princess Anne Civic Center.
